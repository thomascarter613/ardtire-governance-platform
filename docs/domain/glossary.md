# Glossary

Canonical definitions for all domain terms used in the Ardtire Governance Platform.
When these terms appear in code, documentation, or communication, they carry these meanings precisely.

---

## A

**Associate Member**
A registered member of the Ardtire Society with limited participation rights. Associates may observe
deliberation and access published records but do not hold voting rights.

**Audit Log**
The append-only, immutable record of all consequential state changes in the platform. Each entry
records: actor, action, resource, before/after state, timestamp, and active rule version.

## B

**Bounded Context**
A distinct module within the platform with its own domain model, data ownership, and explicit
interfaces to other contexts. Contexts do not share database tables.

## C

**Canon**
The set of officially ratified and published governance documents. Canon documents are immutable
except by formal supersession.

**Constitutional Record**
A ratified governance outcome that carries the force of institutional law within the Ardtire Society.

## D

**Deliberation**
The structured period during which members may comment on and discuss a proposal before a vote opens.
Deliberation has a defined start and end. Its outputs are advisory, not binding.

**Dual Register**
The institutional structure of the Ardtire Society comprising: (1) the public philosophical society,
and (2) the protected Kingdom institution. The platform enforces this boundary at the API layer.

## F

**Full Member**
A member of the Ardtire Society with full participation rights including voting and deliberation.

## G

**Governance Lifecycle**
The sequence of stages a proposal traverses: Intake → Deliberation → Voting → Ratification → Publication.

## O

**Officer**
An appointed member of the Ardtire Society holding a named institutional office with defined
governance responsibilities and platform permissions.

**Outcome**
The formally decided result of a completed vote. Outcomes are binding and reference the rule version
active at the time of the vote.

## P

**Proposal**
A formal submission for governance consideration. Proposals initiate the governance lifecycle.

**Publication**
The act of committing a ratified outcome or document to the official canon. Published records
are publicly accessible and institutionally authoritative.

## R

**Ratification**
The formal approval of a governance outcome by the appropriate authority. Ratification is the final
step before publication.

**Rule Version**
A versioned snapshot of the constitutional rules and governance procedures in effect at a given time.
All governance outcomes are bound to the active rule version at the time of decision.

## S

**Sovereign**
The Head of State of the Kingdom of Ardtire. Holds supreme authority within the Kingdom institution register.

**Standing**
The current status of a member's participation rights — Active, Suspended, Expelled, Pending, etc.

**Supersession**
The formal process by which a canon document is replaced by a newer document. Superseded documents
are retained in the archive and marked as superseded, not deleted.

## T

**Tier**
The membership tier of a member — currently: Associate or Full Member. Tier is one of five factors
in the permission model.
